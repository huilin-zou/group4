Animal Care Centers of NYC (ACC)

How you can help!

All donations go for ACC helps tens of thousands of animals, whether it be at the center or in the community.

When adopting, you are adding them to your family, providing a safe, clean, and loving environment. These homeless pets are finding a new forever home.

The volunteers at ACC all play a vital role within the organization whether it be but not limited to caring for the animals, showing affection, taking photos, or writing entries. There are many opportunities for you to choose from as a ACC volunteer.
